OUR SECRET
We use a nylon micro-fiber material that allows the wallets both to hold more without becoming overstuffed and to fit comfortably in your purse or pants. The non-slip coating prevents your important items, such as your credit cards, from falling out of your wallet.

DURABILITY
Our customers get 4-5 years of life from our nylon line. Our leather-nylon hybrid lines last 5+ years. Unlike regular wallets, Big Skinny wallets feature double-edged stitching which means the material is folded in, glued, and then stitched.

THICKNESS
Our wallets are 10 times thinner and lighter depending on the design and type of leather. Our thin nylon material keeps your wallet at least 50% thinner than regular wallets that have thick leather layers. Most Big Skinny wallets weigh 1 ounce or less versus 3-6 ounces for most regular leather wallets.

COMPOSITION
Our wallets are made with new super-thin nylon micro-fiber material that is lined on the reverse with a non-slip coating to make it harder to lose your cards and cash. Also our nylon-microfiber wallets are 100% Vegan.
